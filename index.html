Nosophobia

In this game you will try and prevent yourself from cobntracting a disease.
Issue is, you dont know what disease.
By using tools availible during an investigation phase you will collect info on the disease you face.
after each investigation phase you will take a preventative measure based on what you think the disease is.
If you remain uninfected you will move onto another investigation phase.
this will loop until you've dodged every transmission method.
Finally you will guess the disease to finalize your triumph.
To help you along the way, you will have access to a book with info on every disease you could possibly be up against.
review it now and then hit the ready button to begin the game.
